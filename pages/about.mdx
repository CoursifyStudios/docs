# About Karasu

Karasu was initially created by [Lukas](https://github.com/quick007) as a part of his 2023 i2 project. In October of 2022, [brandnholl](https://github.com/brandnholl) and [IllGive](https://github.com/IllGive) joined the team and began contributing to the core development.
